来たる9月22日、東京渋谷は"Circus Tokyo"で行なわれるイベント "Interim Report edition2" に出演します。開始17時という社会人の皆様には厳しめの時間から始まって、21時終了という終ったあともう少し遊びに行けそうな時間で終る健全な時間のイベントです。先日、主催者の方とミーティングがありイベントの趣旨など聞かせてもらいましたが、普通のクラブイベントとは一味違うイベントを目指している、そんな実験的なイベントです。

<a href="http://interim-report.org/edition2/">http://interim-report.org/edition2/</a>

I will be performing at "Interim Report edition2" on September 22nd at Circus Tokyo in Shibuya, Tokyo. The event starts at 5 PM and ends at 9 PM -- a reasonable schedule that still leaves room to go out afterwards. I recently had a meeting with the organizer and heard about the event's concept; it aims to be something different from a typical club event -- an experimental event.

<a href="http://interim-report.org/edition2/">http://interim-report.org/edition2/</a>

I'll be performing from 7:30 PM as "dropcontrol/TRIO" in a band format, with Tomohito Yoshijima on drums (who I also performed with in March) and Yasunori Murakita on keyboards (a fellow student of Naruyoshi Kikuchi). Someone might sit in at the last minute as usual (laughs). Please see the member profiles below.

Performing as "dropcontrol/TRIO" with Tomohito Yoshijima on drums and Yasunori Murakita on keyboards.<br />

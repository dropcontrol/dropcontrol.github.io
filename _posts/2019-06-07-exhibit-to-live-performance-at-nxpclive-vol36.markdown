I completely forgot to write about this. On April 20th, I performed a live set at "NxPC.Live vol.36" held at the 21st Century Museum of Contemporary Art, Kanazawa (Theater 21). It was a solo performance, and unusually without any instruments -- I used Ableton Live as the centerpiece, combined with analog synthesizers like the moog mother32 and DFAM, and drum machines like the Elektron Digitakt.

Here's the footage from the performance. Video was recorded by Kohei Nakata ([https://twitter.com/koheinakada](https://twitter.com/koheinakada)), currently an IAMAS M2 student. Thank you! As always, I'm making rather unconventional music, but I hope you enjoy it.

I'm open to live performance offers.
Also, it would make me really happy if you subscribe to the channel!
